# Naming Decision

## Chosen name

- **Namera**
- domain secured: **namera.org**

## Why this name won

- available enough to actually use
- readable and short
- adjacent to naming/renaming without sounding generic
- not tied too narrowly to organizing, downloading, or Plex branding
- broad enough for matching, renaming, tagging, and later destination workflows

## Rejected directions

- plain-English generic names that collided with existing software or domains
- library/shelving names that overemphasized organizing over renaming/tagging
- names too close to FileBot or downloader/piracy tooling vibes

## Outcome

Use **Namera** as the project, repo, and product working name unless a later legal/brand review forces a change.
